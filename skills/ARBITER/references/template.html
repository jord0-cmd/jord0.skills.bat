How It Works

ARBITER is an AI-partnered tool. Paste a contract in the terminal. Claude analyses every clause, risk, dependency, and scenario — then renders it here as a living manuscript.

Reading the Manuscript

  • Red margin bars — high/critical risk clauses
  • Amber margin bars — medium risk, attention needed
  • Dotted underlines — defined terms (hover to see definition)
  • Purple borders — condition gates (if/then logic)
  • Wavy red underlines — dangerous keywords

Interactions

  • Click a clause — expand analysis (risk rationale, market comparison, redline suggestion)
  • Click scenario tabs (left edge) — simulate breach cascades
  • NOV/PRO toggle — switch between plain English and legal text
  • Filter menu — show only specific clause types or risk levels
  • Minimap (right edge) — click to navigate by risk topography

Live Updates

Ask the AI to elaborate on a clause, add a scenario, or re-analyse with a different perspective. The manuscript morphs automatically — no refresh needed.
