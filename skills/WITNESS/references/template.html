Forensic Timeline Reconstructor

How It Works

Paste evidence into the terminal — logs, interviews, reports, financial records. Claude extracts events, maps entities, grades sources, and writes the data. This page renders it as an interactive trench.

Reading the Trench

  • Cyan blocks — machine/system logs (high precision)
  • Amber blocks — human testimony (variable precision)
  • Violet blocks — documents & records
  • Green blocks — visual evidence (CCTV, photos)
  • Sharp blocks = high confidence · Blurry blocks = low confidence
  • Magenta fractures — contradictions between sources
  • Gold dashed lines — causal inferences (Surveyor's Tape)

Interactions

  • Hover blurry events to snap them into focus (the Flashlight)
  • Click events to open the Spectrometer with full source chain
  • Click fractures (left panel) to jump to contradictions
  • Timeline scrolls horizontally through time

"Every event has a witness. Not all stories agree."
